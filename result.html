About Spam

Spam refers to unsolicited or unwanted messages sent over the internet, usually via email. These messages are often sent in bulk and can contain a variety of unwanted content, such as advertisements, scams, and malware. Spam can be a nuisance and can also pose a security risk to users who click on links or download attachments from these messages.
